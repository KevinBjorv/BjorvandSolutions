Smart Indie FAQ

Everything you need to know about how Smart Indie helps beginners finish and release their first game.

What exactly is Smart Indie?

Smart Indie is a step-by-step video program with a community and direct access to me, built to take a beginner from “stuck in tutorial hell” to a finished, released first game without guessing what to do next.

Who is Smart Indie for (and not for)?

It’s for beginners who have tried learning a bit but feel stuck, overwhelmed, or directionless and want a clear path to ship. It’s not for advanced devs, people trying to build an AAA-sized game, or people who only want theory instead of building.

What do you mean by “finish and release a game”?

It means you publish a real itch.io store page and a playable build with a core game loop, so you have an actual released game, not another unfinished prototype.

Which game engine does Smart Indie work for?

The framework works for any engine, and I help you choose the one that fits you best. When I show examples or walkthroughs, I’ll use Unity so it stays clear and practical.

How does the 60-day guarantee work?

If you follow the framework, complete the steps, and follow both the “How to follow the path” guide and the “How to learn effectively” guide, you’re covered by the 60-day release guarantee.

How much time do I need each week to get results?

Plan for at least 6 to 9 hours per week, because consistency beats motivation and the program is built around momentum, not binge-learning.

What support do I actually get after joining?

You get the full video path, the community, personal DMs with me, plus a beta test from me with a short feedback video so you don’t build in the dark.

Do I get lifetime access and future updates?

Yes, it’s a one-time payment with lifetime access and all future updates included, and you don’t need an upsell to succeed, but 1-on-1 coaching is available for students who want advanced help.
