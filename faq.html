Smart Indie FAQ

Everything you need to know about how Smart Indie helps beginners finish and release their first game.

What exactly is Smart Indie?

Smart Indie is a structured video program with community support and direct feedback from me that walks beginner solo game developers from “stuck in tutorial hell” to launching a real first game. Every step is laid out so you know what to do, when to do it, and how to avoid the mistakes that stall most new creators.

Who is Smart Indie for (and not for)?

Smart Indie is designed for beginners and early-stage indie developers who feel overwhelmed, need accountability, and want a clear release path. It’s not the right fit for advanced devs aiming for AAA scope or for anyone looking for theory without building and shipping.

What do you mean by “finish and release a game”?

“Finish and release” means you ship a playable build with a core game loop and publish a live itch.io store page, so you have a completed first game in the wild instead of another half-finished prototype. The program guides you through the production, polishing, and launch steps to get there.

Which game engine does Smart Indie work for?

The framework works with any engine. I help you choose the best fit, and I demonstrate the lessons in Unity to keep the examples practical for most beginners.

How does the 60-day guarantee work?

If you follow the framework, complete the assignments, and use both the “How to follow the path” and “How to learn effectively” guides, you’re protected by a 60-day release guarantee. I’ll coach you through blockers so you can launch on time, and if you don’t release, you get your money back.

How much time do I need each week to get results?

Plan for 6 to 9 focused hours per week. The curriculum is built around momentum, so short, consistent work sessions compound faster than binge-learning and keep your game moving toward release.

What support do I actually get after joining?

You get the full video curriculum, the Smart Indie community, personal DMs with me, and a beta test review with a short feedback video so you always know what to improve next instead of building in the dark.

Do I get lifetime access and future updates?

Yes. Smart Indie is a one-time payment that includes lifetime access, all future updates, and optional 1-on-1 coaching for students who want deeper help. There are no surprise upsells required to succeed.
