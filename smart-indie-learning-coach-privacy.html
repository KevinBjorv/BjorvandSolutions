Privacy Policy

Effective date

December 31, 2025

Overview

This Custom GPT is a learning coach designed to help you learn game development mechanics through active learning. This policy explains what data is and is not collected by the creator of this Custom GPT.

Creator data collection

The creator of this Custom GPT does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data from your use of the GPT. The creator does not operate external servers, analytics, trackers, or databases connected to this GPT, and does not receive your chat messages, files, or account information.

Platform processing

Your messages are processed by the platform you are using to access this Custom GPT. This includes the text you enter, any files you upload, and metadata required to run the service. The platform may store and process conversations according to its own policies, settings, and legal obligations. If you want details on retention, logging, model training controls, and exports, you must review the platform’s privacy policy and your account settings.

Third-party tools and external actions

This Custom GPT does not use external tools, external APIs, or “actions” that send your data to third-party services. If a future version enables external actions, this policy must be updated to clearly describe what data is shared, with whom, and why.

What you should not share

Because this is a chat product, you should avoid sharing sensitive personal information, such as passwords, government IDs, financial account details, or medical information. If you choose to share sensitive information, it may be processed and retained by the platform under its policies.

Changes to this policy

If the Custom GPT is updated in a way that changes data handling, this policy will be updated with a new effective date.
