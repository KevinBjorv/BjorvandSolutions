Smart Indie FAQ

Is Smart Indie right for me if I have never finished a game?

Yes. Smart Indie is built for beginners who have started and quit multiple times. You get a small, finishable plan so you finally ship something real.

Do I need to be good at coding?

No. The program focuses on simple mechanics and repeatable patterns. You only build what you need to ship a small game.

How much time do I need each week?

Even 3–5 focused hours can work. The system is designed for people with jobs, school, or families.

What if I already know Unity but always quit?

Smart Indie gives you scope discipline, deadlines, and accountability so you stop restarting and finally finish.

What if I cannot join right now?

You can still follow my free content on YouTube. When you are ready, Smart Indie will be here to guide you through your first release.
